PennyTrail - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Overview

PennyTrail is a business expense tracking app that operates entirely offline. Your privacy is important to us, and this policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collection

PennyTrail does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The app:

  • Does not require an internet connection
  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not contain advertisements
  • Does not use third-party services that collect data

Data Storage

All data you enter into PennyTrail (expenses, sales, products, inventory, and credits) is stored locally on your device in a private database. This data is not accessible to other apps and is not transmitted to any server.

Data Export

PennyTrail includes an optional CSV export feature that allows you to share your data via your device's share functionality. This action is initiated solely by you, and the app does not automatically share or upload any data.

Permissions

PennyTrail does not request any sensitive device permissions. The app operates entirely within its own private storage space.

Children's Privacy

PennyTrail is not directed at children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us by opening an issue on our GitHub repository.
